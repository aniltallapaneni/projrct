Making High Quality Cancer Care Accessible to Unreached

INDUR CANCER HOSPITAL is a 100 bedded comprehensive cancer specialty hospital in Nizamabad catering to Adilabad, Nirmal, Medak, Kamareddy and Nanded. ICH has state of the art Cancer treatment equipment making advanced cancer care accessible to all sections of society.

INDUR CANCER HOSPITAL offers Medical, Surgical and Radiation Cancer treatments for all types of cancers such as breast cancer, cervical cancer, lung cancer, prostate cancer, skin cancer and leukemia etc.

We have a team of highly qualified and experienced Radiation, Medical and Surgical Oncologists, Radiologists Pathologists, Physicists and other specialist clinicians working collaboratively to bring the best yet affordable cancer care to each patient.

Our infrastructure and resources are holistic, making cancer care affordable to common people who would otherwise have to plan temporary relocation or travel long distances for treatment, undergoing significant physical, financial and emotional turmoil.

About Bridge Gap Hospitals

Bridge Gap Hospitals is spearheaded by a group of young dedicated Oncologists from Hyderabad with an objective to provide comprehensive cancer care services by "REACHING THE UNREACHED".

In pursuit of taking the objective forward, Bridge Gap Hospitals has successfully started a comprehensive specialty cancer care hospital in Nizamabad, covering six districts of Northern Telangana offering treatment for various types of cancer such as breast cancer, cervical cancer, lung cancer, prostate cancer, skin cancer and leukemia etc.

Cancer treatment is very expensive and beyond the reach of many people. There is no structured, scientific and cost effective model to deliver the cancer treatment to the 700 million strong middle class population of the country till date.

The ideal mix of Oncology professional (Doctors) and Management professional in the board of the company is setting the right direction for growth of our project.

Cancer Diagnosis at End Stage

Cancer is a primary cause of death across the world and three quarters of the patients in India come to hospitals with end stage cancer. There are more than 100 types of cancers and one million plus cancer cases are diagnosed every year claiming over 7,00,000 lives making it the second leading cause of death.
